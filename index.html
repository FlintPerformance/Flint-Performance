FAQ

COMMON
QUESTIONS

How does the coaching work?
After signing up you complete an onboarding intake form covering your goals, schedule, training history, and lifestyle. Your coach builds your custom plan and you're rolling within a few days.
Can I switch levels later?
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at the end of any billing period. We want you in the tier that actually fits your life — not locked into something that doesn't serve you.
What equipment do I need?
Whatever you have access to. Full gym, home setup, or minimal equipment — your plan is built around your actual situation, not an idealized one.
How much time do I need per week?
As little as 3 hours a week can produce serious results. Programs are built around your availability — not the other way around.
What if I travel a lot for work?
We plan for it. Your program includes travel-friendly alternatives so you never fall off — hotel gym, airport layover, or nothing but a hallway.
How do the multi-month discounts work?
When you choose a 3, 6, or 12-month plan you pay the full term upfront in a single charge — that's why the rate is lower. The longer the commitment, the bigger the discount: 5% for 3 months, 10% for 6, 15% for 12. You can see the exact amount due before you enter any payment info.
